Abroad 1 More Time Games - 2025

Co-designed with Danilo Valente

A tableau-building game in which you travel across Europe visiting 240+ unique locations, trying to plan the best trip.

Each day you spend in a region counts for area majority scorings. Combine that with a race to complete bucket lists, go to festivals and combo your cards off one another, and you've got a slick optimization game with much more interaction than usual.

I had the idea for Abroad while planning a trip and realizing you need to think carefully to save money and energy while still visiting all places you want to go.

It's my most ambitious project so far, and it cemented my partnership with Danilo, with whom I worked for 6 months almost exclusively to get it ready.
