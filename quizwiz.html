QuizWiz Brain Games - 2025

A coop trivia game in which you'll allocate countries from your hand in the categories you think they belong.

QuizWiz is a trivia game with endless replayability. It's ok to repeat the categories, because there are special, modular ones, with answers that depends on which regular categories are in play.

It's a difficult coop game, in which players don't need to know the answers, but they need a lot of lateral knowledge to get them right.
