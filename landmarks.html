Landmarks Floodgate Games - 2024

Co-designed with Danilo Valente

A cooperative word association game with a giant twist: the game starts with only 3 words on a map, and the clues you give will be used to build paths to the treasures.

While the paths expand, the new words can also serve as a base for the next clues you give.

Landmarks is my first partnership with Danilo Valente. After he showed me his latest prototype, we started riffing off ideas until the basic concept for Landmarks was born.

After an online pitch, the Floodgate folks fell in love with it and decided to publish it. A few more tweaks, including a new competitive mode, an amazing production, and Landmarks was ready to go.
